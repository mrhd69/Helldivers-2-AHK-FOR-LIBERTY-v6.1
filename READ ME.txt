*After interseption is installed*

Open monitor check box your keyboard then press any key you will see a number between 1-20 on the left everytime
 you click that is your keyboardID.
 
next 
open up KeyboardID.ini  and infront of KeyboardID= put your keyboardID then you can save and exit.

now just load up For Liberty V6.1 and your done. make sure when you put your keybinds in you press update and 
save that way you can load them later on.
